Welcome to a simple demo of my physics library (Work in Progress)


The following are some quick demos to demonstrate the functionality of the library


Central Attraction Demo: All Particles are the Same Mass, Collisions and Central Attraction

Collision Demo: Two Particles of Identical Masses With a Central Attraction and Collision (both are constantly accelerating towards the center)

Closed System Demo: Collision with Static Boundaries, no Inter Particle Collisions. 3000 particles with random mass/size.

Billiards Demo, Particles arranged like billards with another particle collding with all of them.

Cradle Demo, Particles arranged a Newton's cradle, Inter Particle Collisions and Collisions with Boundaries left and right of Particles.
